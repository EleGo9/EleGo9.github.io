About

I am an AI Engineer at Hipert Srl, working on computer vision and perception systems for autonomous and robotic applications. My work focuses on 3D scene understanding — spanning 6D pose estimation, monocular 3D object detection, and robotic manipulation pipelines.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, where I previously worked as an AI researcher at HiPeRT Lab. I am passionate about knowledge sharing and community building: I have taught AI to industry professionals, co-organised international workshops, and conducted research as a visiting PhD student in Munich.
